Mid-Year Update & First Japan Trip

Written: 2023-06-17

Visit Back to Tokyo and New Shoes

The next day I went to the closest mall and wandered around hoping to find a shoe store since my shoes were completely worn down at this point. They stunk like crazy, were damp even a day later, and were extremely dirty. However it took a few hours of hunting since every couple steps my feet were aching from the (unknown to me at the time) blisters on my feet. I eventually bought new shoes and just found the nearest trash can and dumped my old ones. On my way back I stopped by a McDonald’s and tried out the Teriyaki Chicken sandwich, which was what I needed at the time since I could take all the comfort foods that I could get.

I spent a day mostly relaxing after the cat island trip and getting new shoes since I felt so exhausted. I also didn’t want to damage my feet any further by walking around more. The next day came and it was the last day I had before I went to the airport, so I made the adventure back to Akihabara to make sure I scouted everything there to get for friends and family, and made a good haul. I found a crane game that had plushies from a YouTube channel my friend and I like watching called からめる. As well as a few Kirby ones, vtuber keychains for myself, and other knickknacks. I was tempted to buy some NieR soundtracks but held myself back because of the price, but still thought it was cool to hold them in my hands physically.

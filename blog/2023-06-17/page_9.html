Mid-Year Update & First Japan Trip

Written: 2023-06-17

Conclusion

Although not a perfect vacation (I don’t recommend sleeping outside in November), I’d say overall it was a fun experience. It was a taste of Japan before moving there and it gave me the learning opportunity on how to navigate the train system, shops, and other skills needed to live there. I know traveling for vacation isn’t the same as moving somewhere but at least I have the know-how of navigation before I take the jump to move.

I already have a flight and hotel booked this year to visit Osaka this year as a (hopefully) final trip before I move there by the end of next year. I plan to visit 姫路城 (Himeji Castle), 奈良公園 (Nara Park), and possibly some other locations. I wasn’t able to visit any of my former schoolmates while they were living there abroad but I still like seeing picture from them regardless. On top of that trip I’m already also planning a trip to visit Europe early next year. My reason being is that if I’m moving to Japan by the end of 2024 I might as well take vacation time that I have in my current company to visit somewhere else and not Japan again.

My ideal plan would to visit Cairo, Athens, and Rome for a historical journey around Europe. But it’s not concrete yet and it’s still an idea rattling in my head. I do want to visit every continent at least once in my life. But that’s all for me, I hope to have a fun 2nd trip and improve my Japanese over the course of the year!


My personal favorite snack in Japan. Pumpkin Pie flavored!

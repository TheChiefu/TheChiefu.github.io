A New Year’s Update

Written: 2018-01-01

Happy New Year for those who use the Gregorian Calendar! I just wanted to post this update to share how progress has been going on Taurus and how everything in my life is going relating to it.

​The question still remains though, “How’s Taurus been? What’s the progress”; which I’d respond “Good question! Let me show you!”. Away from the hypotheticals the game has been going great, it’s still in ​setup​ phase as I call it since the structure of the game is still in progress.

So since after summer ended, I got a job working as a Bus Monitor & some nights after work I’d go to my college classes. This for the most part has left me drained hence why no posting or updates on Taurus. for months.. well that’s because I wasn’t working on it; at all. Although I want to work on the game most days I’m just not motivated enough to work on it. However don’t fret, I go through these phases where I go from intensely working on my projects for a month straight, everyday and every hour I’m awake to doing absolutely nothing for a month or two. Luckily since I took my “break” in September the down time has given me new ideas for game-modes, how to structure a story in the game, and other new elements. So I thought, “Hey, it’s the beginning of a new year! Why don’t I try to improve Taurus, and get over the hurdle of burning myself out”. Burning myself out by spending too much of my time on a project is really my Achilles-Heel and I’m making an effort to just get over it.

​ So not to end on a incompetent ramble here are some ideas I have for Taurus in the future:

  • Multiplayer
    • Objective Modes:
      • Capture: Capture enemy points
      • Fortify: Defend your team’s points until timer runs out
      • Destroy: Eliminate enemy team’s points
    • Team Deathmatch: (5v5, 4v4, 3v3) Eliminate as many enemy players until score limit or time runs out.
    • Wing-man: 2-Man Team, get as many kills as possible until time runs out. Team-mates as stronger when close to each other.
    • Free For All: Get to kill limit or until time runs out
    • Supremacy: Large-Scale battle with capture/destroy/fortify objective team-based combat. Get to point limit or until timer runs out
    • Juggernaut: One player gets boss-like ship while others have to defeat boss before timer runs out. The boss player has to eliminate all enemy player lives.
  • Story:
    • Main Story: Unaltered version of story, plays as normally intended.
    • Secondary Story: Play main story but with all upgrades (for fun mode)
    • Boss Gauntlet: Fight bosses one after another with a few health packs and all ship upgrades but no other help.

Also since I’m a big fan of Halo’s Community aspects, I want to implement in Custom player game-modes since it theoretically shouldn’t be that bad to implement. Just make public player traits to edit, and save them to a file. But only time will tell…

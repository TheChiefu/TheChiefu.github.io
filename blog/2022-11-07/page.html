Introduction

After writing numerous posts on this site about myself I’ve come to realize how diverse my interests and life actually is. I don’t mean this as a boast but a realization since whenever I had to write in school about myself or introduce myself to new people I usually just say the same things, “I like computers, games, and nature”. Which although true is really non-descriptive about my personality and who I am as a person.

The reason I’d introduce myself or talk about myself in that way was because I never knew how to talk about myself. I never really organized my thoughts in a way to clearly talk about who I am and delve into what makes me “tick” so to speak. So this is what this post is trying to dig up from my thoughts and create a cohesive image of well… me. I won’t go into every detail on trying to self-realize, but since I’m relate this post into my trip to Japan this will talk about how I got into Japanese media/culture, why I like an maintain an interest in it, and how it’s shaped me and my life.

A good way to describe my personality as a whole would be “Half American” and “Half Japanese”.

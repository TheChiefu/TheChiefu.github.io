Reflection: Going to Six Flags with my Family

Another experience that I had over the summer was attending a trip to Six flag with my two cousins and my uncle. We went for the entire day, visiting the water park and the main park. I only did the water park because by the time we got out of the water park by the evening I was already burnt out. I got to try water slides I’ve never tried before, like The Funnel which is a giant funnel that 4 people spin around until they ride to the bottom of the tunnel. It was a fun new experience and the sights were amazing.

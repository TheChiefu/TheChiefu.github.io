Chariho Summer

Expectation 10: Respect for Others

Experience: Helped my father Clean Pools and Help People Out

Even though I didn’t have a full-time summer job (due to not actively looking for one) I worked some days with my Father on pools, helping him out and helping others out with their problems. I didn’t ask for payment but the $20 when I went was enough; it gave me time to work and talk with my father on a basis I usually don’t do that often, and it gave me the opportunity to work in a new environment that I hadn’t experienced before. I got to see all types of people and see how a work environment really is. One pool we helped clean was my grandmother’s pool (the house I stayed at for the summer) and it made everyone happy because it is the only family pool. So my cousins and I got to swim in the pool on Sundays and since some of my cousins were in 2nd grade they stayed in the pool all day long, until we forced them out. An activity most people including myself would usually do when swimming in the pool.

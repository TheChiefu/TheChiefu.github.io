Who am I and what are my Interests?

My name is Austin Lanphear and I am an 11th Grade Student at Chariho High School who has an Interest in Technology and Science. My leisurely activities consist of playing video games (alone or with friends), watching online videos such as YouTube, learning about science and game development, and going outside to relax or work on a project.

Science has always been a big factor in my life because it reveals how our universe works and that to me has always been intriguing and inspiring. Computers have always been apart of my life ever since I was young, and I believe it helped me gain a technological advantage over my peers because I used it from a very young age and not many people at the time had access to them, it also gave me a new hobby. Anime was introduced to me from Cartoon Network and ever since then I occasionally watch a show or two for entertainment and a source of relaxation, anime is one of the many reasons why I have an interest in Japanese culture.

Video Games, are the defining factor on who I am. They have always been my main source of entertainment and why I have an interest in game design. Video Games gave me an interest in orchestral music, Japanese culture (Nintendo), and more subjects. Outdoors has always been important to be, it mixes with science and my love for astronomy, it gives me relaxation and a goal to reach when working on projects outside such as a campsite.

Conclusion:

That wraps a mini autobiography of myself, I hope it gives you a general insight on why I made the decisions I did over the summer for each expectation.
